Student union

As a Chalmers PhD student you have the right (and the obligation) to be part of the Student Union. Sign up to the Student Union, this automatically gives you 3 months in the SGS queue and gives you access to all student discounts Mecent. The fee is 250 SEK fee every semester.

** Student union card is REQUIRED for taking an exam ** NOTE: If you are a Chalmers PhD student, in order to graduate you need to prove you have payed the fee to the Student Union for all the semesters you have work at Chalmers.
